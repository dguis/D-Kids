DKIDS Games


Fun Center

Fun Center is a game, containing a few minigames. These games include MadLibs, a Frog Maze, and Balloon Pop. Instructions are in the game.

Star Hunter

Star Hunter is a game where you are a cat with a net, trying to catch stars in the deep of the ocean. Collect as many stars as you can without touching an octopus. Touch an octopus, it's game over. Press the green flag to try again.

Underwater Adventure

Underwater Adventure is an upgraded version of Star Hunter. The concept is pretty much the same, but instead of clicking the green flag, simply hit play.

Fish Destruction

Use the arrow keys to avoid dangerous obstacles. But there's a catch. After you go forward, you can't go back. And you can't touch the edge of the screen.

Spider Crawl

Once in Spider Crawl, press space to begin. Use the arrow keys to navigate a series of mazes and challenges, avoiding the green, always trying to touch the yellow.
